Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist, teacher, and the 11th President of India. He played a leading role in India's missile and space programs and inspired millions of students through his vision and dedication.

Major Achievements

1931

Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.

1969

Joined ISRO and contributed to satellite launch vehicle projects.

1998

Played a key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests.

2002

Became the 11th President of India.

2015

Passed away while delivering a lecture to students.
